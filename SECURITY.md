# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in ClawRun, please report it responsibly. You can:

1. Use **GitHub's private vulnerability reporting** by going to the [Security tab](https://github.com/clawrun-sh/clawrun/security) of the repository and clicking "Report a vulnerability"
2. Or email **support@clawrun.sh**

**Please do not open a public issue for security vulnerabilities.**

## What to Expect

- We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours
- We will investigate and provide an initial assessment within 7 days
- We will work with you to understand and resolve the issue before any public disclosure

## Scope

This policy covers the ClawRun project and all packages published under the `@clawrun` npm scope, as well as the `clawrun` and `zeroclaw` packages.

Third-party dependencies are outside the scope of this policy. If you find a vulnerability in a dependency, please report it to the maintainers of that dependency directly.

## Credit

We are happy to credit reporters who follow responsible disclosure in our release notes, unless they prefer to remain anonymous.
